# User Guide - Wonder Mentorship Homework Partner

Wonder Mentorship is a free, AI-powered homework helper for students in grades 3-8. This guide covers how to use the platform as a **student** and as a **parent**.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Open the app in your browser and click **Sign Up**.
2. Enter your **full name**, **email**, and **password**.
3. Choose your role: **Student** or **Parent**.
4. Click **Sign Up** to create your account.

Already have an account? Click **Log In** instead.

### Linking a Student to a Parent

Wonder Mentorship connects students to their parents using a simple **join code** system:

1. **Parent** signs up and goes to **My Children** in the navigation bar.
2. Parent clicks **Add a Child**, entering the child's name and grade level.
3. A unique **6-character join code** is generated (e.g., `ABC123`). Share this code with your child.
4. **Student** signs up for their own account, then goes to the **Join Your Family** page.
5. Student enters the join code to link their account.

Once linked:
- The student's name and grade auto-fill on homework and practice pages.
- All homework and practice activity is saved and visible to the parent.
- Parents can generate weekly reports based on real data.

## Student Portal

### Grade My Homework

Use this feature to check your completed homework and learn from your mistakes.

**How to use it:**

1. Navigate to **Grade Homework** from the student home page.
2. If your account is linked to a parent, your name and grade are filled in automatically. Otherwise, enter your **name** and select your **grade level** (3-8).
3. Choose how to submit your homework:
   - **Type It** - Type or paste your problems and answers into the text box.
   - **Upload a Photo** - Take a photo of your homework sheet and upload it. The AI will read your handwriting or printed text directly.
4. Click **Check My Work**.
5. The AI will grade each problem and show you:
   - Whether your answer is **Correct** or needs another try
   - A friendly **explanation** of what happened
   - A **hint** to help you improve

If your account is linked, your results are saved automatically so your parent can see your progress.

**Tips:**
- Write one problem per line for best results when typing.
- For photo uploads, make sure the image is well-lit and the text is readable.
- Supported photo formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP (max 10MB).
- You can submit word problems, equations, fractions, and more.

---

### Practice Problems

Use this feature to get extra practice on topics you want to master.

**How to use it:**

1. Navigate to **Practice** from the student home page.
2. If linked, your name and grade are filled in automatically. Otherwise, enter your **name** and select your **grade level**.
3. Pick one or more **topics** you want to practice by clicking the topic buttons:
   - Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division
   - Fractions, Decimals, Percentages
   - Algebra, Geometry, Word Problems
4. Click **Generate Problems**.
5. You'll receive 5 practice problems tailored to your grade level.
6. Try solving each problem on your own first, then click **Show Solution** to see the answer and a step-by-step explanation.

**Tips:**
- Start with one topic at a time for focused practice.
- Try to solve each problem before peeking at the solution.
- Generate a new set of problems anytime you want more practice.

## Parent Portal

### Dashboard

The parent dashboard shows an overview of all your linked children, including:

- **Sessions** - How many homework submissions they've made
- **Problems** - Total number of problems graded
- **Accuracy** - Overall percentage of correct answers

From the dashboard you can navigate to manage children, view weekly reports, or support the program.

### Manage Children

Use this page to add children and manage their join codes.

**How to use it:**

1. Navigate to **My Children** from the navigation bar.
2. Click **Add a Child** and enter their name and grade level.
3. A unique **6-character join code** is generated. Share this with your child so they can link their account.
4. Once your child enters the code, their status changes to **Linked** and all their activity will appear in your dashboard and reports.

You can add multiple children. Each child gets their own join code.

### Weekly Report

Generate a summary of your child's learning progress for the week. Reports are based on **real homework and practice data** saved from your child's activity.

**How to use it:**

1. Navigate to **Weekly Report** from the parent dashboard.
2. Select which **child** you want the report for.
3. Optionally add any **strengths** or **areas to work on** you've noticed (comma-separated).
4. Click **Generate Report**.
5. The AI pulls your child's actual grading and practice data from the past 7 days and generates:
   - A written **progress summary** of the week
   - **Highlights** of your child's achievements
   - **Suggestions** for how to support their learning at home

### Support Wonder Mentorship

Wonder Mentorship is a nonprofit program. The donation page explains the impact of your child's usage and offers a way to support the platform.

**How it works:**

1. Navigate to **Support Us** from the parent dashboard.
2. Read the impact message showing how the platform helps your child.
3. If you'd like to contribute, choose an amount: **$5**, **$10**, or **$25**.
4. You'll receive a personalized thank-you message showing the impact of your donation.

**Note:** Donations are entirely optional. The platform is and will remain free for all students regardless of donation status. Students never see donation-related content.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is this free?**
Yes. Wonder Mentorship is a nonprofit and the platform is free for all students. Optional donations from parents help cover operating costs.

**Q: What subjects are supported?**
Currently, the platform focuses on **math** for grades 3-8, including arithmetic, fractions, decimals, percentages, basic algebra, geometry, and word problems.

**Q: Does my child see donation messages?**
No. The student portal is completely separate from donation content. A built-in safety filter ensures students only see educational material.

**Q: How accurate is the grading?**
The AI tutor is highly capable but not perfect. It works best with clearly written problems and answers. We recommend parents periodically review graded homework for accuracy.

**Q: Do I need an account?**
Yes. Students and parents each create an account. Parents add their children and share a join code to link accounts. This allows progress tracking and weekly reports based on real activity.

**Q: Is my child's data stored?**
Yes. Homework grading results and practice sessions are saved to your child's profile so parents can track progress over time. Data is stored securely in Supabase with row-level security, meaning each family can only access their own data.

**Q: Can I have multiple children on one parent account?**
Yes. Go to **My Children** and add as many children as you need. Each gets their own join code and separate progress tracking.

**Q: Can my child use this on a phone or tablet?**
Yes. The app is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and computers.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| "Something went wrong" error | Refresh the page and try again. If it persists, the AI service may be temporarily unavailable. |
| Grading seems incorrect | Try reformatting your homework more clearly, with one problem per line. For photos, ensure good lighting and legible handwriting. |
| Join code doesn't work | Make sure you're entering the code exactly as shown (6 characters, letters and numbers). Codes are case-insensitive. |
| "This code has already been used" | Each join code can only link to one student account. Ask your parent to check the **My Children** page. |
| Weekly report shows no data | Your child needs to have completed at least one homework or practice session in the past 7 days. |
| Page won't load | Check your internet connection. Clear your browser cache if needed. |
| Slow responses | AI responses typically take 5-15 seconds. Photo uploads and longer homework may take a bit more time. |
